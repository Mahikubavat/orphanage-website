Volunteering at orphanages in India is crucial for providing vulnerable children with love, care, and support, contributing to their development and overall well-being, while also offering volunteers a chance to make a meaningful impact and gain valuable skills.

One of the main impacts of orphanage volunteer work in India is the emotional support provided to the children. Many of these children have experienced trauma and loss, and having volunteers who show them compassion and affection can help them heal and build trust again.

Volunteering helps counteract the effects of stress, anger, and anxiety. The social contact aspect of helping and working with others can have a profound effect on your overall psychological well-being. Nothing relieves stress better than a meaningful connection to another person.
