Celebration of Festival

Celebrating festivals in orphanages fosters a sense of belonging, cultural awareness, and joy, helping children develop social skills and a positive outlook on life, while also promoting community engagement and shared experiences.

Celebrating festivals is an important part of our children's lives at . We believe that these celebrations help our children develop a sense of cultural identity, and create happy memories that they will cherish for years to come.

Celebrating different festivals allows children to learn about various cultures and traditions, broadening their horizons and fostering a sense of global citizenship. By participating in these celebrations,children can develop a deeper understanding of their own heritage and cultural identity.
